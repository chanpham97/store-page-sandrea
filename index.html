What is Akidna?

Akidna is a Record Label that specializes in small Artists with talent and skill. We are an NYC based label that promotes the entertainment industries next big Artists. Unfortunately, these smaller, talented Artists usually end up replicating their favorite idol, usually as a way to pay their idol homage, and end up becoming their idols "knock-off" or "wanna-be". Akidna aims to help these artists. If you want to support our brand, follow our socials and continue to navigate our website.
